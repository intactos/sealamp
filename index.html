Set up your lamp

Follow these steps to connect your lamp to WiFi:

1 Swipe down from the top of your screen and connect to the seazencity WiFi network. No password needed.
2 Tap the button below to open the lamp setup page.
3 Choose your home WiFi and enter the password.
4 Switch your phone back to your home WiFi, then come back here.
